SWAPSTERS

Have you ever wondered how people traded before invention of currency?
Have you ever wondered how people bought stuff when there was no money?
Have you wondered how easy it could be if we could just trade something we don’t need for something we actually need?
Well, we have a solution. How about we show you that its possible now?
We are trying to make it simpler to trade. No money, no transaction. Just exchange.
You have something sitting in your garage that you no longer need but don’t know what to do with it? Just post it on SWAPSTERS.
You see something on here that you might like? Contact the person directly and get it exchanged.
Its that simple! No money transfers, no bank account or credit card information required. Just plain old way of trading.
